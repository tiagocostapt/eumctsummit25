UA92 in Manchester

University Academy 92 (UA92), situated in the bustling area of Old Trafford, Manchester, is not only a pioneering higher education institution but also a prime conference venue. Nestled in the iconic building that once housed Kellogg’s, UA92 boasts state-of-the-art facilities including a spacious events suite, a 146-seater auditorium, and versatile break-out rooms, tailored for a diverse array of events and conferences. The campus is a stone’s throw away from the Old Trafford tram station, ensuring seamless connectivity to Manchester’s extensive transport network.
UA92's journey began in September 2017, born from a partnership between Lancaster University and the Class of '92, a group of esteemed Manchester United footballers, with a vision to offer education that transcends traditional academic boundaries. The institution emphasizes personal growth and employability, preparing students for real-world challenges. A significant aspect of UA92's ethos is its collaboration with industry leaders to enrich the educational experience. Microsoft, as a key partner, plays a vital role in integrating technology and innovation into both the curriculum and the conference facilities. This partnership ensures that UA92 remains at the forefront of digital advancements, providing cutting-edge resources for students and conference attendees alike. The involvement of Microsoft underlines UA92’s commitment to delivering a modern, tech-driven environment conducive to learning, professional development, and event success.
Manchester is a dynamic city in the heart of the United Kingdom known for its rich industrial heritage and vibrant cultural scene. As the birthplace of the industrial revolution, Manchester has transformed into a hub of creativity, innovation, and sports. The city boasts an impressive array of museums, galleries, and theatres, alongside a thriving music and nightlife. Visitors can explore the historic architecture, indulge in diverse culinary delights, and experience the legendary friendliness of Mancunians. With excellent transport links and a packed calendar of events, Manchester is a city that invites exploration and promises memorable experiences for all who visit.
European MCT Summit 2025 logo is based on the bee icon, which is a proud emblem of Manchester, symbolizing the city’s industrious past and its citizens’ hard work ethic. Adopted during the Industrial Revolution, the worker bee represents Manchester’s role as a hive of activity and enterprise. It’s featured on the city’s coat of arms and is scattered throughout Manchester, from buildings to street art, signifying the community’s resilience and unity. The bee has gained even more significance following the 2017 Manchester Arena bombing, embodying the city’s spirit of solidarity and defiance in the face of adversity.

Transport

Manchester and UA92 itself is well served by transport links.
Google maps is usually very reliable for planning journeys by public transport around the UK, but here are some tips and more details to get you started.
Here are some options for visitors to get to University Academy 92 (UA92) from central Manchester train stations and from Manchester Airport. See also https://ua92.ac.uk/contact-us/find-us/

Using Public Transport:

  • By Tram from Manchester City Center: The Metrolink tram service runs regularly to Old Trafford tram station, which is about 100 meters from the UA92 campus. You can find times and routes on the Metrolink website.
  • By Bus from Manchester City Center: Several bus routes link the campus with Manchester city center, including the 278 and X50. Find more timetables and route details over on www.tfgm.com.
  • By Train: Manchester Piccadilly, the main rail station, is just over a 10-minute drive from UA92. All Manchester city center train stations are also well served by public transport and taxi services.
  • By Plane: The closest international airport is Manchester Airport which is approximately a 20-minute drive away from the UA92 campus. From here, you can connect onwards by taxi, train or shuttle bus. If you are arriving in the UK at a different airport then Manchester is well connected by train.

From Manchester Airport (20 minutes/9 miles):

Information on travel to/from Manchester Airport is available on their official website https://www.manchesterairport.co.uk/getting-to-and-from/
  • By Taxi: Licensed taxis are available at all terminals and the train station at the airport. UBER is also bookable in the Manchester cosmopolitan area.
  • By Train: There are train services that connect the airport to Manchester city centre, from where you can transfer to a tram or bus to reach UA92.
  • By Shuttle Bus: Shuttle services are available that can take visitors from the airport to various points in the city.

By Car:

Visitors can use the postcode M16 0PU for sat-nav directions to UA92, located on Brian Statham Way.
