About European MCT Summit

The European MCT Summit is the premier event designed exclusively for Microsoft Certified Trainers (MCTs). This annual gathering serves as a dynamic platform where training professionals come together to share knowledge, foster connections, and stay ahead in the ever-evolving field of Microsoft technologies.

Our summit offers a unique blend of keynote presentations, in-depth sessions, and hands-on workshops led by industry experts and Microsoft representatives. Attendees will have the opportunity to explore the latest advancements in Microsoft products, gain insights into new training methodologies, and enhance their technical skills.
